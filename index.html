About me

Robotics, Computer Vision & AI

MSc Robotics & Automation (UPM, TUD). BSc Electronics & Automation (UPM).
ex Airbus Defense & Space Embedded Software Engineer (A3R-MRTT). Publications and a patent in wearables/IoT.

Robotics & Autonomous Systems Computer Vision Event-based Vision Machine Learning Deep Learning Generative AI (LLM, VLM, Diffusion) Embedded Systems IoT


I design and build intelligent systems that merge AI, robotics, and embedded hardware. My last research focuses on the development of a self-supervised learning algorithm for 3D prediction of logistic objects using event-based vision, combining cutting-edge computer vision with efficient dataset creation. This work leverages event-driven data and NeRF-inspired models to significantly improve annotation efficiency and prediction accuracy.

Beyond academic research, I am naturally curious and passionate about creating my own projects. I have explored areas such as 3D printing, process automation, and applied artificial intelligence, always looking for ways to transform ideas into tangible solutions. That said, I am currently seeking opportunities to contribute to research in cutting-edge companies, with the ambition of pursuing an Industrial PhD or similar program alongside my professional career.

In addition, I hold a patent derived from my Bachelor Thesis and have collaborated on multiple research projects in Spain and Germany, strengthening both my technical expertise and my international perspective.


Languages: Spanish (native), English (C1), German (A2).
